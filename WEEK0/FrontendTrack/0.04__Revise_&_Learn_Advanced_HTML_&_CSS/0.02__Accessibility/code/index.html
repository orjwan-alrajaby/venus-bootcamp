Palestine — Tatreez Embroidery
Palestinian embroidery patterns historically represented village identity, family heritage, and social symbolism.
Yemen — Oral Poetry
Yemen has strong oral poetry traditions used in storytelling, celebrations, and public gatherings.
Lebanon — Mezze
Lebanese mezze includes shared dishes such as hummus, tabbouleh, kibbeh, and labneh.
Algeria — Couscous
Couscous is considered one of the most important staple foods across Algeria and wider North Africa.
Iran — Persian Mosques
Persian architecture is known for geometric tilework, domes, courtyards, and symmetry.
Yemen — Tower Houses
Sana'a is famous for multi-story tower houses decorated with white gypsum patterns.
Oman — Wadis
Oman contains many wadis: valleys that can fill with seasonal water.
Algeria — Sahara Desert
Much of Algeria is covered by the Sahara, the world's largest hot desert.
